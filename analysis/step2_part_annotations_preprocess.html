This markdown was written for kiddraw_annotations (data originally collected in Long, Fan, Chai, & Frank, 2021). The data and figures of this markdown will be submitted to the CogSci conference 2022.

Note about what happened before this markdown: 1) data collected from the experiment through analysis/step2_generate_metadata/kiddraw_generate_metadata.ipynb

  1. data was preprocessed in analysis/step3_analysis/kiddraw_analyses.ipynb in particular, data sessions were excluded based on low accuracy on the catch trials and we used this notebook to check whether every drawing has responses from at least 3 annotators.
